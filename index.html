FAQs

🎮 So what actually is Highlife Games?

It's a quiz game with a twist! You become an Account Controller, answer sponsored questions, collect cool assets, and race to become Account Director. Oh, and you can win real prizes. Ages 14+.

🏆 How do I win?

Grab one Asset Item from each of the ten categories, stack up the most Star Credits, and reach Account Director status. Then you bag a full Five-Part-Ticket to the knockout comps!

🧠 Do I need to be a genius?

Nope! The game balances question difficulty so everyone's on a level playing field. You need brain power and a bit of luck — that's it!

🎁 Can I actually win real stuff?

Yes! Winners get a full Five-Part-Ticket to knockout competitions with real prizes. Even 2nd and 3rd place earn ticket parts you can trade online.

💰 What on earth are HECUs?

Highlife Entertainment Currency Units — your in-game money! You start with 50,000 and earn more by nailing questions. Spend wisely!

👫 Can I play with my mates?

Absolutely! Trade ticket parts, gift game items, and earn bonus points for bringing friends. Five-player games get you free complete tickets too!
